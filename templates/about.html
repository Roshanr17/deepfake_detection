About Deepfake Detector

Deepfake Detector is an advanced AI-powered system designed to identify synthetic or manipulated video content. Using state-of-the-art deep learning techniques, our system analyzes video frames to detect signs of deepfakes with high accuracy.

As deepfakes become increasingly sophisticated, having reliable detection tools is crucial for maintaining trust in digital media. Our detector is continuously improved and trained on the latest datasets to stay ahead of deepfake creation techniques.

Technology & Features

Deep Learning

Uses advanced neural networks trained on diverse datasets to identify deepfakes with high accuracy.

Fast Processing

Analyzes video content quickly, providing results in seconds for real-time detection capabilities.

Confidence Scoring

Provides detailed confidence metrics showing the probability of content being authentic or manipulated.

Easy to Use

Simple web interface allows anyone to upload and analyze videos without technical knowledge.

Privacy Focused

Your uploaded videos are processed and not stored for security and privacy protection.

Continuous Improvement

Regular model updates ensure detection remains effective against new deepfake techniques.

How It Works

Our detection system uses a combination of techniques:

  • Frame Extraction: Video is divided into key frames for analysis
  • Face Detection: Identifies and focuses on facial regions
  • Feature Analysis: Extracts features related to facial artifacts and inconsistencies
  • Classification: Uses trained neural networks to classify content as real or fake
  • Confidence Calculation: Provides probability scores for final predictions
